Build Strength Safely

Form, tempo, and recovery basics.

Strength training is a marathon, not a sprint. Prioritize technique, control the tempo, and match volume with recovery.

1) Lock in your form

Start lighter, groove the pattern, and progress weekly. Filming sets helps.

2) Control tempo

Use a 3-1-1 tempo on big lifts to build stability and mind-muscle connection.

3) Recover hard

Sleep 7–9 hours, hit your protein, and schedule deloads.
